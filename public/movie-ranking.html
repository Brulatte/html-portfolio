The Best Movies According to Johanna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Man From Snowy River

This is a great cowboy movie about loss, love, proving oneself, and working hard to reach your goals.

Spirit Stallion of the Cimarron

I've loved this one since I was a kid. The music by Hans Zimmer is incredible and this horse's resilience is to be admired.

The Holiday

Christmas themed movie with romance, comedy, and Jack Black.
